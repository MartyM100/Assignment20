"It takes a Village!"

East Flatbush Village Inc. - over a decade of uplifting and developing our community! Since 2008 enriching lives and inspiring youth for a brighter and promising future.

A Community and Youth Development Organization focused on uplifting Neighbors with Neighbors. Our organization combats violence within the community by providing these children with recreational activities and educational tools. Building them a strong foundation which they will carry with them into adulthood.
